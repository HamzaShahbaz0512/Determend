Our Story – A Family's Journey to Transform Tragedy into Purpose

A Family's Determination

In 2012, our world changed forever when our three sons and cousins—Archie, George, and Isaac—were diagnosed with a terminal disease. Following Archie's death in 2022 at just 16 years old, his courage and determination to live fully became the foundation for what DETERMEND represents today.

His twin brothers, George and Isaac, will turn 16 this year. Every day with them is a gift, and every day we work to build something that honours not just Archie's memory, but the thousands of families walking similar paths.

DETERMEND isn't just a company or a brand. It's a promise.

A Brand Built Out of Necessity

DETERMEND wasn't born in a boardroom. It emerged from the darkest moments of our lives. Following Archie's death at 16, we faced a choice: let grief consume us, or channel it into something that could help other families walking the same impossible path.

DETERMEND exists because it had to. And now it exists for YOU.
